LEAD COURSE INSTRUCTOR
Seminar in Ecology – Environmental Biology (BIOL5430) – California State University Los Angeles – 2022
I was the instructor for this course on advanced study of selected topics in methods of data analysis during the Spring Semester of 2022. I centered the class around “Plants and Climate Change”, with an emphasis on plants responses to drought. I designed the syllabus and the course structure for the semester, which involved lectures I taught to introduce fundamental concepts applied to existing challenges, students’ oral presentations, written assignments, and in-class discussions of papers.
